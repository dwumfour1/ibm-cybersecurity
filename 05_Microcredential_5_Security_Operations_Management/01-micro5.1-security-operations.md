# Micro 5.1: Security Operations

## About This Learning Activity
In this module, you'll explore how organizations manage and maintain security through **Security Operations Centers (SOCs)**.

You will learn how security operations are structured and implemented:

- Understand different **SOC models** such as in-house SOC, virtual SOC (V-SOC), SOC as a Service (SOCaaS), and hybrid SOC  
- Learn the roles and responsibilities of **SOC teams**  
- Explore **security awareness training** and how it helps reduce human-related security risks  
- Understand how organizations build effective training programs to improve overall security posture  

---

## Learning Objectives
By the end of this module, I am able to:

1. Distinguish between different security operations models  
2. Identify key tasks performed by SOC teams  
3. Assign roles and responsibilities within a Security Operations Center  
4. Apply types of security awareness training to real-world scenarios  

---

## Lessons

- Security Operations Center (SOC)  
- Security Operations Center Teams  
- Security Awareness Training

## Reflection
This module helped me understand how organizations structure their **security operations to monitor, detect, and respond to threats**. Learning about SOC models and team roles showed how important coordination and clear responsibilities are in maintaining security.  

It also highlighted the importance of **security awareness training**, reinforcing that human behavior plays a critical role in cybersecurity.
